Thank you Tucson!

Three good renewable candidates are making it on the November ballot for Arizona Corporation Commission! They are Ylenia Aguilar, Joshua Polacheck, and Jonathon Hill.

You can support the clean elections campaigns of two of the candidates by donating $5 through the Arizona Secretary of State's portal for Ylenia Aguilar and Jonathon Hill. Your donation will help these candidates get state funding. You can support Joshua Polacheck by donating directly to his campaign website.

Mission Statement

GTCC's mission is to create a unifying voice in the community, representing our full diversity, for climate policies and action to achieve electrification, decarbonization, energy efficiency and adaptation to climate impacts in our region.

The Greater Tucson Climate Coalition is a group of citizens and community groups, in southern Arizona, that self organize to advocate for climate solutions.
