Sustainable Leadership Training

Four Saturdays, 10 am to noon

January 6, 13, 20, 27

Online via Zoom

Brush up your skills for action in the New Year. Here are four workshops to support professional effectiveness, improve problem solving, and build the sustainable community.

  • Jan 6. Meeting Essentials
  • Jan 13. Equitable & Inclusive Leadership
  • Jan 20. Negotiation and Problem-solving
  • Jan 27. Peer Coaching

These free workshops are for people who want to make a palpable difference in the quality of life for Tucson and beyond: citizen activists, neighborhood leaders, business members, and civic officials. Each workshop stands on its own, but we encourage taking the whole series. Donations are optional, but welcome.
